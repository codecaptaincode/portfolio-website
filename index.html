About Me

Hello! I’m Sienna Shepherd, an aspiring software engineer located in NYC. I’m currently learning essential tools and technologies to embark on my journey in software development. For as long as I can remember, I have always been interested in how technology works and how to work on solutions for problems I’ve encountered. What I love about software engineering is the intersection of technology with creativity and logic. My goal is to create meaningful and impactful technologies that help improve people’s lives.

I have a varied work experience, from customer service jobs to hospital work. I believe my diversified background is an asset, as I’ve learned how to work with all sorts of folks and can quickly adapt to any situation that arises. Even though I am just getting started in the tech field, I already have a drive to learn as much as I can. I approach challenges with curiosity, and grow from my struggles and missteps. My desire to make a difference with the work that I do is powerful, and I look forward to honing the skills I learn into real solutions.

When I am not learning code, I enjoy exploring the vibrant city that surrounds me in NYC. I find inspiration from the energy all around. From the diverse groups of people, the amazing food and events, and being able to be my true self, I wouldn’t want to be anywhere else. I love cozying up on the couch with a good book or video game, and I greatly enjoy creating music, as I have just started playing guitar along with writing songs. Playing with my dog, Bear, also brings me joy.
